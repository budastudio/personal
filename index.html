About & Philosophy

I am Leonardo M. Scarcia, Senior Creative Designer specialized in Brand Identity, Motion Graphics and Digital Experiences.

With more than 20 years of international experience, I have collaborated with agencies and brands including Mormaii, Neokai, Cellfish Media, TBWA Worldwide, The Jeffrey Group and United Virtualities.

My approach combines strategy, creativity and technology to transform ideas into meaningful visual experiences.

I believe great design is the balance between clarity, emotion and purpose.
